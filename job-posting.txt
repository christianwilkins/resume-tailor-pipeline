Quant Trading Associate Job Description  

The Role 

The Quant Trading Associate (QTA) role is our entry-level position within our Trading and Quant organization and gives you broad exposure to the whole business as you work towards graduating from the Associate Program and assuming a role on a team as a Quantitative Trader. 

As a QTA, you will work in a high-impact role as a member of a trading team. You will play a vital part in identifying trading opportunities and communicating with traders, risk managers, software engineers, and quants throughout the trading day. You will learn all aspects of the trading operation and work towards a role that generates trading revenue for the team. In addition to the trading and quantitative side of the business, you will have exposure to technology through our combined trading and technology curriculum. 

We encourage competitiveness and collaboration in equal measure and strive to better each other to win as one team. 

What to Expect  

Due to the dynamic nature of this business, we cultivate an environment of continuous learning and growth through an in-house education department. Through formal classroom work, mock trading, and real-time practical exposure, you will gain a holistic introduction to the derivatives market while sharpening your quantitative skillset. You can expect: 

    An introduction to options pricing, theory, trading philosophies, and programming courses
    Exposure to different product areas and responsibilities
    One-on-one mentorship from senior members in the firm
    Opportunities to grow quickly and start making autonomous trading decisions
    Participation in data-driven research for the purpose of alpha generation 

What it takes for you to be successful: 

    You have a competitive drive and are a highly motivated individual that only gets stronger as part of a collaborative team
    An aspiration to collaborate with traders, quants, developers, and other experts to further CTC's goal of providing world class liquidity to financial markets
    You love solving complex problems
    You're intellectually curious and like to challenge the status quo
    You can effectively communicate sophisticated ideas both written and verbally
    Strong quantitative and analytical abilities
    Basic programming skills or a willingness to learn Python
    No prior experience in finance is necessary, but a deep curiosity and a hunger to learn is essential
